Bread and butter

Description

The bread and butter is, as the name suggests, a go-to breakfast for most people, especially for students and workers alike. It doesn't take much effort to create, yet still makes for a delicious treat in the morning.

    Ingredients

  • Loaf of bread
  • Butter

    Instructions

  1. Use a knife or spoon to scoop butter
  2. Put the spoonful of butter into the bread
  3. Mix it around the loaf of bread
  4. Fold it in half and enjoy the meal!
