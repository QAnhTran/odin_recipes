Instant noodles

Description

Instant noodles is another famous form of breakfast, especially in university students. Not only is it cheap to make, it also has very good taste for its price. Relying on it too much may give you a stomachache, however.

    Ingredients

  • Pack of instant noodles
  • Boiling water

    Instructions

  1. Open the pack of noodles and put the noodles themselves and supporting ingredients into a bowl
  2. Pour boiling water into the aforementioned bowl, then put a cover on top of it
  3. Wait for approximately 5 minutes, take out the cover and enjoy the food!
