Fried egg

Description

The fried egg is a versatile food, since one can choose to eat it on breakfast, lunch, or dinner. It is best served with fried rice or bread.

    Ingredients

  • Raw egg
  • Pepper
  • Cooking oil

    Steps

  1. Start boiling the frying pan and pour cooking oil on it
  2. Break the egg by hitting it against a hard physical object(i.e. knife, table) into the frying pan
  3. Cook it for 5 minutes, then flip it over and repeat the same step
  4. Once finished, stop boiling and put the egg onto a dish, and you're done!
